17 ### Description
18 
19 * Handles **linear and quadratic interpolations** (tetras, hexas, triangles, quads) and various Gauss quadratures.
20 * Handles essential and natural boundary conditions, deformation dependent natural conditions, with or without forcing terms.
21 * Most of the problems (arteries, asme, cee530, nrl) are **stochastic boundary value problems** where the elasticity tensor or strain energy function are **modeled as random fields**.
22 * Deterministic problems are also considered in some subfolders.
23 * The c++ classes defining the boundary value problems (i.e., **linearizedElasticity**, **compressibleHyperelasticity** and **nearlyIncompressibleHyperelasticity**) are pure virtual classes, **independent of the constitutive equations** (random or deterministic elasticity tensor, random or deterministic strain energy function), boundary conditions, etc.
24  * __linearizedElasticity__: see folders asme, cee530
25  * used in asmeSBVP, ceeSBVP, linearPatchTest and manufactured
26  * __compressibleHyperelasticity__: see folder nrl
27  * used in compressibleHyperelasticity_linearPatchTest, manufacturedSolution, rubberblock, tiMooney, tiMooneyRandomField
28  * __nearlyIncompressibleHyperelasticity__ (three-field formulation with static condensation): see folder arteries
29  * used in dirichletInletOutlet_PolyconvexHGO, dirichletStripElongation_StochasticPolyconvexHGO, neumannInnerSurface_PolyconvexHGO, neumannInnerSurface_StochasticPolyconvexHGO
30 * Also solves Laplace's equation for the generation of fibers directions in the folder arteries.

36 ### Example: solving a boundary value problem in linearized elasticity
37 
38 * The pure virtual class **linearizedElasticity** has six virtual methods, namely:
39  * **get_neumannBc**: returns your natural boundary condition while assembling the force vector
40  * **get_forcing**: returns your forcing vector while assembling
41  * **get_elasticity_tensor**: returns your elasticity tensor
42  * **get_elasticity_tensor_for_recovery**: returns the elasticity tensor for post-processing the solution, useful for stochastic boundary value problems
43  * **setup_dirichlet_conditions**: builds the essential boundary conditions that you want to apply
44  * **apply_dirichlet_conditions**: apply your essential boundary conditions
45 
46 * You need to construct your own class object inheriting from **linearizedElasticity** and which contains at least the six virtual methods.
47 
48 * Examples can be found in e.g. **src/asmeSBVP.hpp**, **cee530/sbvp/ceeSBVP.hpp**, **cee530/linearPatchTest/linearPatchTest.hpp** and **cee530/manufactured/manufactured.hpp**
